About Hindu YUVA

Our Mission

HINDU YUVA aims to create a vibrant and inclusive platform for students at UMass Boston to celebrate, promote and engage with Hindu culture, traditions and values. Through cultural events, educational initiatives and collaborative activities, we strive to foster unity, diversity and understanding within the university community.

Our Vision

HINDU YUVA believes in the philosophy of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam, which translates to "The world is one family." This belief emphasizes unity and interconnectedness among all individuals. The club also upholds the value of hospitality, guided by the principle of Atithi Devo Bhava, meaning "Guest is God," ensuring a warm and welcoming environment for all.
